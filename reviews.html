● Review Insights & Recommendations
☕
Peanut Butter Latte is Your Brand Ambassador
Mentioned in 18+ reviews across both platforms, the PB Latte consistently drives 5-star ratings and repeat visits. Multiple reviewers call it the "reason I keep coming back." Consider featuring it as the #1 recommended first-time order on menus and delivery platforms.
⏰
Speed of Service is the #1 Complaint
Wait time issues appear in 8 reviews, including the only 1-star Google review for SJ. Two patterns emerge: (1) weekend afternoon rushes (Fred Rodriguez waited 30 min at 2 PM Saturday), and (2) orders being lost/never called out (Franchesca Flores, Jacqueline Esquivel). An order-ready notification system or number displays would address both.
💰
MV Has a Value Perception Gap
Price sensitivity is concentrated at Mountain View (electrikblue1008, Mathew Miller, Gene D.). Meanwhile, SJ's Tiffany T. initially felt the same but changed her mind after tasting. Consider visible messaging about premium ingredients (oat milk free, hand-whisked matcha) and portion justification -- the coffee is Vietnamese-strength, intentionally concentrated.
🌟
Off-Menu Magic Drives Loyalty
Jason Wang's 5-star review for MV specifically highlights staff suggesting an off-menu pandan cream matcha. Kristine F. returned twice for blueberry matcha after a staff recommendation. Train team to proactively suggest off-menu combos -- it creates memorable experiences that generate glowing reviews.
🍺
Beer Selection is an Untapped Review Driver
Veronica P.'s most detailed SJ reviews focus on craft beer flights and sour pairings. Multiple reviewers mention beer but few have tried it. The craft beer + coffee combo is genuinely unique in the market. Consider "Coffee & Craft" flight experiences or beer-focused events to generate more reviews about this differentiator.
